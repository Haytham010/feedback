⭐ Board Members & Medical Faculty – Al-Nahda University Hospital

Al-Nahda University Hospital is proud to include an exceptional team of academic and medical leaders who work together to ensure the delivery of high-quality healthcare and medical education. The collaboration between the Hospital Board of Directors and the Medical Faculty plays a key role in enhancing clinical skills, developing the healthcare system, and supporting scientific research.

🔷 First: Hospital Board of Directors

The Board of Directors is responsible for setting the hospital's strategic policies, monitoring performance, ensuring the delivery of safe medical care, and strengthening the hospital's role in medical education and training. Board Structure

• Chairman of the BoardOversees the hospital's overall vision and directs development and expansion plans.
• Dean of the Faculty of MedicineResponsible for coordinating academic and educational activities within the hospital and strengthening integration between the faculty and hospital operations.
• Chief Executive Officer (CEO)Manages daily operations and ensures the quality of both medical and administrative services.
• Director of Medical AffairsSupervises all medical departments and monitors the performance of healthcare staff.
• Director of Finance & AdministrationResponsible for financial resources, budgeting, and managing staff affairs.
• Director of Nursing AffairsDevelops nursing policies and oversees nursing teams across the hospital.
• University or Community RepresentativeSupports the hospital's mission as an educational and community-centered healthcare institution.

🔷 Second: Medical Faculty

The Medical Faculty represents the core foundation of both the academic and clinical missions of the hospital. It includes professors, consultants, and specialized physicians who supervise student training, provide high-quality medical care, and contribute to scientific research. Structure of the Medical Faculty

• ProfessorsProvide advanced clinical and academic training and lead scientific research teams.
• Associate ProfessorsSupervise postgraduate education and oversee clinical training programs.
• Assistant ProfessorsContribute to daily educational activities and clinical services across departments.
• ConsultantsManage advanced and complex medical cases and supervise specialized units.
• SpecialistsRun outpatient clinics, emergency services, and inpatient wards.
• ResidentsReceive continuous hands-on clinical training under the supervision of senior faculty.
• InternsRotate across different departments to gain practical experience before graduation.
