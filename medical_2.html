Medical Accreditations

First: Medical Accreditations

1) Medical Quality Accreditation (Local/University Accreditation)

The hospital has received quality accreditation from authorized university bodies after meeting key standards, including:

  • Patient safety
  • High-quality clinical care
  • Efficiency of medical administration
  • Compliance of all departments with unified hospital policies and procedures

2) Infection Control Accreditation

Thanks to the implementation of modern infection-control systems, the hospital has obtained official accreditation confirming:

  • Compliance with international sterilization standards
  • Regular training for medical and nursing staff
  • Use of advanced disinfection and sterilization technologies

3) Medical Education Quality Accreditation

The hospital has been accredited as an official teaching center for medical students, providing:

  • Advanced clinical training
  • Supervision by specialized academic staff
  • State-of-the-art medical skills laboratories
Second: Awards & Achievements

1) Excellence in Healthcare Award

The hospital earned this award for:

  • Rapid development of medical infrastructure
  • Providing high-quality healthcare services
  • Implementing digital transformation systems for appointment management and medical records

2) Excellence in Community Service Award

Awarded in recognition of the hospital's medical outreach programs and healthcare campaigns serving the local community.

3) Major Medical Achievements

  • Performing the first advanced laparoscopic surgery during the hospital's first year
  • Establishing a comprehensive Critical Care Center
  • Training the first cohort of medical interns using an advanced simulation-based system
Third: Al-Nahda Hospital's Commitment to Quality

Al-Nahda University Hospital operates under a vision that focuses on:

  • Continuous improvement
  • Ongoing training for medical and administrative staff
  • Applying the latest global best practices
  • Regular review of procedures and clinical protocols

The administration aims to obtain additional accreditations, such as:

  • National healthcare quality accreditation
  • Digital patient safety standards
  • Accreditation for radiology and laboratory units
