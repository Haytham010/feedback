⭐ History & Establishment – Al Nahda University Hospital

Founded in 2025, Al Nahda University Hospital was established as a strategic step within the university's vision to enhance the healthcare sector and elevate the quality of medical services provided to the community. The hospital was created to serve as an integrated medical and educational platform, combining high-quality healthcare services with the training of medical students and the development of healthcare professionals in accordance with the highest standards.

From its early beginnings, Al Nahda University Hospital started with a strong foundation that included modern specialized clinics and advanced diagnostic units equipped with state-of-the-art medical technologies. This enabled the hospital to deliver accurate medical services under the supervision of a distinguished team of professors and consultants.

Since its inauguration, the hospital has adopted a model that integrates medical care, education, and research, making it a leading center for graduating doctors with solid academic knowledge and strong clinical skills.

As community needs evolved, the hospital expanded gradually to include new units such as:

  • Emergency & Reception Unit
  • Fully equipped Operating Rooms
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU)
  • Multidisciplinary Outpatient Clinics
  • Surgical Operations and Follow-up Center
  • Advanced Laboratories and Radiology Departments

Today, Al Nahda University Hospital stands as one of the region’s modern medical institutions, thanks to its advanced facilities, highly qualified medical team, and integrated healthcare management system. The hospital continues to pursue its vision of becoming a leading medical and educational center locally and regionally—by providing safe, high-quality healthcare, advanced medical education, and supporting scientific research that benefits the community.
